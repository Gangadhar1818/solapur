Shri Siddheshwar Temple

Solapur is a city and district in the state of Maharashtra, India, known for its textile industry, particularly its handloom and powerloom weaving. It's also recognized for its historical significance, including a brief period of self-rule before India's independence. The district is a cultural confluence with Marathi, Telugu, and Kannada languages spoken.
